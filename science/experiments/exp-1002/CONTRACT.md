# CONTRACT INPUT

**Document:** Master Services Agreement
**Parties:** DataStream Solutions, Inc. ("Vendor") and Meridian Federal Partners, LLC ("Client")
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the Effective Date by and between DataStream Solutions, Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Vendor"), and Meridian Federal Partners, LLC, a Virginia limited liability company ("Client").

---

### 1. DEFINITIONS

**"Deliverables"** means any work product, software, documentation, reports, or other materials created by Vendor specifically for Client under a Statement of Work.

**"Intellectual Property Rights"** means all patents, copyrights, trademarks, trade secrets, and other proprietary rights.

**"Losses"** means any and all losses, liabilities, damages, costs, and expenses, including reasonable attorneys' fees and court costs.

**"Services"** means the professional services, software, and related deliverables provided by Vendor as described in one or more Statements of Work issued under this Agreement.

**"Statement of Work"** or **"SOW"** means a written document executed by both parties describing Services, Deliverables, timelines, and fees.

---

### 2. SERVICES AND DELIVERABLES

**2.1** Vendor shall perform the Services described in each SOW in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards.

**2.2** Client shall provide Vendor with timely access to personnel, systems, and information reasonably necessary for Vendor to perform the Services.

**2.3** Vendor may use subcontractors to perform portions of the Services, provided that Vendor remains responsible for subcontractor performance and compliance with this Agreement.

---

### 3. FEES AND PAYMENT

**3.1** Client shall pay Vendor the fees set forth in each SOW within thirty (30) days of receipt of a valid invoice.

**3.2** Fees not paid within thirty (30) days shall accrue interest at the rate of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is lower.

**3.3** All fees are exclusive of applicable taxes. Client is responsible for all sales, use, or similar taxes arising from the Services, excluding taxes on Vendor's income.

---

### 4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**4.1 Pre-Existing IP.** Each party retains ownership of all Intellectual Property Rights in materials developed prior to or independently of this Agreement ("Pre-Existing IP").

**4.2 Deliverables.** Subject to full payment of all fees, Vendor hereby assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in and to the Deliverables, excluding any Vendor Pre-Existing IP incorporated therein.

**4.3 License to Vendor IP.** To the extent any Vendor Pre-Existing IP is incorporated into the Deliverables, Vendor grants Client a non-exclusive, perpetual, royalty-free license to use such Vendor Pre-Existing IP solely as incorporated in the Deliverables.

---

### 5. CONFIDENTIALITY

**5.1** Each party ("Receiving Party") shall hold in strict confidence all non-public information disclosed by the other party ("Disclosing Party") that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure ("Confidential Information").

**5.2** The Receiving Party shall use Confidential Information solely to perform its obligations or exercise its rights under this Agreement, and shall not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without the Disclosing Party's prior written consent, except to employees or contractors who have a need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as those in this Agreement.

**5.3** Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for a period of five (5) years.

---

### 6. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

**6.1** Each party represents that it has full authority to enter into this Agreement and that doing so does not violate any other agreement to which it is a party.

**6.2** Vendor represents that the Services will be performed in a professional manner consistent with applicable industry standards, and that the Deliverables, as delivered, will not knowingly infringe the Intellectual Property Rights of any third party.

**6.3** EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY SET FORTH IN SECTION 6.2, VENDOR MAKES NO WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, OR NON-INFRINGEMENT. THE SERVICES AND DELIVERABLES ARE PROVIDED "AS IS."

---

### 7. TERM AND TERMINATION

**7.1** This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues until terminated as provided herein.

**7.2** Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon sixty (60) days' written notice to the other party.

**7.3** Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days of written notice thereof.

**7.4** Upon termination, Client shall pay Vendor for all Services performed and expenses incurred through the effective date of termination. Sections 4, 5, 8, 9, and 10 shall survive termination.

---

### 8. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

**8.1 Cap on Liability.** EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN SECTION 8.2, IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE TO THE OTHER FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, EXEMPLARY, PUNITIVE, OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES OF ANY KIND, INCLUDING LOST PROFITS OR LOSS OF DATA, REGARDLESS OF THE FORM OF ACTION AND WHETHER IN CONTRACT, TORT, STRICT LIABILITY, OR OTHERWISE, EVEN IF ADVISED OF THE POSSIBILITY OF SUCH DAMAGES. FURTHER, EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN SECTION 8.2, EACH PARTY'S TOTAL CUMULATIVE LIABILITY TO THE OTHER ARISING OUT OF OR RELATED TO THIS AGREEMENT, WHETHER IN CONTRACT, TORT, OR OTHERWISE, SHALL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL FEES PAID OR PAYABLE BY CLIENT TO VENDOR IN THE TWELVE (12) MONTHS IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING THE EVENT GIVING RISE TO THE CLAIM.

**8.2 Exceptions.** The exclusions and limitations of liability in Section 8.1 shall not apply to:

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(a) either party's indemnification obligations under Section 9 of this Agreement;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(b) either party's breach of its confidentiality obligations under Section 5 of this Agreement;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(c) infringement, misappropriation, or violation of either party's Intellectual Property Rights;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(d) damages arising from a party's gross negligence or willful misconduct; or

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(e) amounts owed by Client for fees, expenses, or taxes under Section 3 of this Agreement.

---

### 9. INDEMNIFICATION

**9.1 Vendor Indemnification.** Vendor shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Client and its officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any and all third-party claims, actions, suits, proceedings, and any resulting Losses arising out of or related to: (a) Vendor's performance of the Services; (b) any allegation that the Deliverables infringe the Intellectual Property Rights of a third party; (c) Vendor's breach of this Agreement; or (d) the gross negligence or willful misconduct of Vendor or its subcontractors.

**9.2 Client Indemnification.** Client shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Vendor and its officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any and all third-party claims, actions, suits, proceedings, and any resulting Losses arising out of or related to: (a) Client's use of the Deliverables in a manner not authorized by this Agreement; (b) Client's breach of this Agreement; or (c) the gross negligence or willful misconduct of Client.

**9.3 Procedure.** The indemnified party shall: (a) promptly notify the indemnifying party in writing of any claim; (b) grant the indemnifying party sole control of the defense and settlement; and (c) provide reasonable cooperation at the indemnifying party's expense. The indemnifying party shall not settle any claim in a manner that imposes liability or obligation on the indemnified party without prior written consent.

---

### 10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

**10.1 Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of law principles.

**10.2 Dispute Resolution.** Any dispute arising out of or related to this Agreement shall be resolved by binding arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association under its Commercial Arbitration Rules. Arbitration shall take place in Wilmington, Delaware. The arbitrator's decision shall be final and binding and may be entered as a judgment in any court of competent jurisdiction.

**10.3 Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, together with all SOWs, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior and contemporaneous agreements, representations, and understandings.

**10.4 Amendment.** This Agreement may not be amended except by a written instrument signed by authorized representatives of both parties.

**10.5 Waiver.** No failure or delay by either party in exercising any right under this Agreement shall constitute a waiver of that right.

**10.6 Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

**10.7 Notices.** All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by email with confirmation, overnight courier, or certified mail to the addresses on file for each party.

**10.8 Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.
