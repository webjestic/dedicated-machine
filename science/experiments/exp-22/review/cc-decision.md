# Exp-22 CC Decision

**Date:** 2026-03-30

## Objection Status: Accepted

The Mechanism Resolution Confound is accepted as a fatal objection. Exp-22 changed two
variables simultaneously: pragmatic force AND vocabulary specificity. We cannot isolate
which variable drove the 10/10 detection rate.

## Conclusion (Narrowed)

**Established:** Interrogative artifact with mechanism-level causal vocabulary drives detection
at ceiling (A=10/10, B=10/10, C=0/10). This demonstrates that the artifact CAN be a
detection vector when the vocabulary is specific and the framing is interrogative.

**Not established:** Whether pragmatic force (interrogative vs assertional) is the operative
variable, or whether mechanism-level causal vocabulary drives activation regardless of framing.

## What Remains from This Experiment

1. **Pragmatic force theory** (exp-21a + exp-22): assertional artifact → 0/10; interrogative
   artifact → 10/10. This pattern is real, but may reflect vocabulary specificity rather than
   framing.
2. **H2 corroborated again:** A=B at 10/10. Slot irrelevant when artifact vocabulary drives
   detection. (Caveat: at ceiling.)
3. **Directed vs independent detection:** exp-22 is directed (model answers a described scenario);
   exp-23 is independent (model discovers from vocabulary cues). This distinction matters for
   practical implications.
