Q2: **May 1** — Delivery on March 17 triggers the exception clause (after the 15th), so the 30-day period runs from April 1, the first business day of the following month, landing on May 1.

Q4: **Vendor B is performing better at the project-category level** — this is Simpson's Paradox: Vendor A's superior overall rate is an artifact of its portfolio being weighted toward small projects (where both vendors perform well), not evidence of superior execution, and per-category performance is the operationally honest comparison.
